2. What TETA+PI provides

TETA+PI is a verification registry that enables entities (businesses, journalists, artists, and other registered entities) to obtain a cryptographically verifiable identity profile. Our services include:

  • Entity verification against official registries (Handelsregister, EU VAT, GLEIF, DE-ÖKO, and others)
  • C2PA content signing credentials for verified entities
  • Bitcoin timestamping of verification records
  • A public API and MCP server for querying verified entities
  • A user interface at app.tetapi.dev for managing your verified profile

4. Verification accuracy

TETA+PI performs verification by querying official third-party registries. We do not independently audit every piece of data returned by those registries. Registry data may occasionally be outdated or incorrect. If you believe a verification record is inaccurate, contact us at hello@tetapi.dev.
