The algorithm

Ranking earned through trust. Not purchased through ads.

TWIRA(entity, query) = α·T + β·I + γ·P
T
Trust Score
Verification level × recency × source weight. Registry attestation counts more than self-declaration.
I
Intent Alignment
Semantic match between the agent's query and your blocks. C2PA-signed blocks weighted higher.
P
Provenance
Bitcoin timestamp depth + C2PA chain + endpoint uptime. History cannot be bought — only accumulated.
Co-invented with our CTO. arXiv paper in preparation.
The formula is public. The registry data behind it is not.
