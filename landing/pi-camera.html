How it works

From capture to proof in seconds.

01
Open PI Camera and shoot

The app uses your phone's camera. You see a live indicator that C2PA signing is active. No settings needed.

02
C2PA manifest is generated

Immediately on capture: your identity, timestamp, GPS, and device fingerprint are embedded in a signed C2PA manifest inside the file.

03
File hash anchored on Bitcoin

The file's SHA-256 hash is sent to OpenTimestamps and committed to Bitcoin. Proof is independent of TETA+PI servers.

04
Content goes live on your MCP endpoint

Your verified profile is updated. AI agents querying teta_verify_entity now see the content and its full proof chain.
